# STELLAR NEIGHBORHOOD

**STELLAR NEIGHBORHOOD** is a *Spaceflight Simulator* custom solar system that expands the restricted horizons of both sun system and places to visit.
Now the player will be in one of many star systems around Sagittarius A* and its cluster of stars, having the freedom to explore all the places that this proyect offers.

All the systems are made with scientific precision, unofficial impressions and controversial\debatable scientific publications around certain objects, there are celestial bodies who have custom terrain for the objective of having more variety and landmarks that drive the player's curiosity for exploring.

---

# FEATURES

- An extended solar system with unique landmarks, landscapes and gameplay value.
- Day & night cycles to most important celestial bodies that have atmosphere.
- Rings with particle-like texture for most gas giants, includin the shepherd moons that orbit there in the expansion folder.
- Realistic stars with their coronas.
- visual sunflare for the inner solar system.
